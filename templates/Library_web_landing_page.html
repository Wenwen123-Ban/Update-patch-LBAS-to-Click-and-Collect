About LBAS

Library Borrowing and Assistance System

LBAS is a web-based Click & Collect Library Reservation System developed to modernize how students access library resources at Northwestern Mindanao State College of Science and Technology.

Instead of physically going to the library just to check if a book is available, students can search, reserve, and schedule a pickup online — reducing waiting time, minimizing crowding, and making library services more accessible whether on campus or at home.

For librarians, LBAS provides tools to manage book inventory, track reservations, approve borrowing requests, and monitor the collection — replacing manual record-keeping with a faster and more organized digital workflow.

This system is part of an undergraduate research study on digital transformation in academic library services, grounded in the Technology Acceptance Model (TAM) and Information Systems Theory.

Developed by
LBAS Research Group — NMSC-ST, BSIT
